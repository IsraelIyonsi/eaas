💡 Navigation Design Decision

Inbound emails live under the existing "Emails" section (as a "Received" tab alongside "Sent") rather than a separate top-level "Inbound" section. This keeps the mental model simple: Emails = everything about email (sent + received). Inbound Rules get their own top-level section because they are a configuration concern, not an email viewing concern.
